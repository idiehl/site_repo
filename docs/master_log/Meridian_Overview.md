## Concept
Atlas Meridian is a next-generation desktop canvas for visual knowledge mapping, creative
systems, and media-rich editing. It focuses on spatial organization, linked nodes, and
an intuitive, fast canvas experience.

- Establish a clear workflow and unique differentiation.
