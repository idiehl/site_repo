Eazy Does It is a platform for artists, creatives, leaders, & business owners in Austin, TX. Our goal is the preservation of culture & the introduction of unique perspectives through storytelling. Eazy Does It is the ear to the ground of what is current amidst our culture here in Austin, TX, and celebrates individuals that are contributing to the betterment of our city in unique ways. Now we are expanding on a national & global level to celebrate artists contributing to their communities. By using our voices and stories to inspire action far and wide we create a sense of relatability between strangers on platforms that we frequent everyday.
